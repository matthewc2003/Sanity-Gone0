Mudrock is a powerful self-sustaining defender packing both offensive and defensive power. She has a high upfront DP cost, but makes up with high overall stats and a kit that helps her take pressure and sustain herself. Most notably, she has high Max HP and ATK stats compared to the rest of the defender class, HP being especially important since all of her self-heals scale off of max HP.
Her kit is adaptable to fit different situations, whether it be constant rapid pressure or slow hard hitting pressure from enemies, you can bring a different skill to deal with it without much need of outside help. Mudrock can be effectively used both as a major damage dealer or tank, being a powerhouse in a lot of difficult content to date, and when used properly can be nigh-invulnerable against some of the toughest enemies.
